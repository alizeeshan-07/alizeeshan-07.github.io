My work pairs **systems engineering with provable guarantees** (CMDP / Lyapunov / finite-time bounds). Selected directions:

- **Agentic RL** — training LLM agents rather than only prompting them: RLVR, process reward models, multi-turn credit assignment, tool-use RL, long-horizon reward design, and the underlying convergence/sample-complexity theory.
- **Inference-time compute allocation** — *when to think, how long, on which model*: test-time compute as a budgeted decision problem, bandit/RL routers, cascades, early-exit, budgeted MDPs.
- **Safe & verifiable autonomy** — CMDPs, Lyapunov/ISS, shields/CBFs, MPC filters, runtime monitors, and conformal-prediction guarantees for learning-based control.
- **Online / continual adaptation of deployed agents** — online LoRA/PEFT, continual RL, two-timescale slow-weights/fast-policy analysis, drift detection.
- **World models & learned simulators** — RSSM/Dreamer-class and transformer/diffusion dynamics for model-based RL and what-if planning.
- **MARL for multi-agent LLM systems** — cross-agent credit assignment, joint training, and mechanism design.
- **Efficient inference (system-level)** — KV-cache compression, speculative decoding, MoE offloading, and quantization effects on reasoning.
- **AI-native 6G / AI-RAN** — DRL/MARL schedulers, LLM intent layers, GNNs, and digital twins, aligned to the 3GPP Rel-20/21 and ITU IMT-2030 window.
- **Federated / distributed training of agents** — federated LoRA/PEFT and decentralized optimization for device and agent fleets.
- **Security of agentic systems** — adversarial RL, robust shields, anomaly probes, and red-team benchmarks.

**Interest areas:** LLMs · Quantization · IoT + AI + Wireless · MARL · Edge AI · Digital Twins · Agentic / GenAI · Collaborative & Distributed AI · World Models · Agent Safety & Verifiable Autonomy · ISAC & Semantic Communication · Agentic RL & Test-Time Compute · Security of Agentic Systems.
